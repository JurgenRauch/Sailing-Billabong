Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance, especially during peak season (December-April). However, we often have last-minute availability.

What's included in the tour price?

All tours include professional crew, safety equipment, snorkeling gear, refreshments, and lunch on full-day trips. Specific inclusions vary by tour.

Do you offer private charters?

Yes! We offer custom private charters for groups of 2-12 people. Perfect for special occasions, corporate events, or intimate family adventures.

What if the weather is bad?

Safety is our priority. If weather conditions are unsafe, we'll reschedule your trip or provide a full refund. We monitor conditions closely.
